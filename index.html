Chun-Ju Lai (賴俊儒)

cjlai@mpim-bonn.mpg.de

Starting Sep 2020, I will be an assistant research fellow at the Academia Sinica . Before that, I was a limited term assistant professor at the University of Georgia ; a visiting postdoc fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn, Germany. I obtained my PhD at the University of Virginia under supervision of Weiqiang Wang .

My research interests include quantum groups, affine Hecke algebras, modular representations, geometric representation theory and algebraic combinatorics.
