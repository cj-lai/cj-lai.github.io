Chun-Ju (CJ) Lai (賴俊儒)

cjlai@gate.sinica.edu.tw



I've joined the Academia Sinica as an Assistant Research Fellow since Sep 2020. I was a limited term assistant professor at the University of Georgia ; a visiting postdoc fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn, Germany. I obtained my PhD at the University of Virginia under supervision of Weiqiang Wang .

My current research interests include affine Hecke algebras, quantum groups, quantum symmetric pairs, modular representation theory, quiver varieties, Springer fibers, geometric representation theory, categorifications, and algebraic combinatorics.
