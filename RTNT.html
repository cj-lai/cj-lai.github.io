Representation Theory in Northern Taiwan (RTNT)

We are a group of representation theorists (in the broad sense) from Academia Sinica, National Central University, National Center of Theoretical Sciences, National Taiwan Normal University, National Taiwan University(to be confirmed) and National Tsing Hua University(to be confirmed). We run an informal weekly learning seminar on representation theory and related topics.

Potential Future Topics

  • Categorification: An introduction to diagrammatic algebra and categorified quantum sl(2) + more
  • Geometric representation theory: Perverse Sheaves and Applications to Representation Theory
  • Geometric representation theory: Representation theory and complex geometry
  • Lie superalgebras: Dualities and Representations of Lie Superalgebras + more
  • Koszual duality: Koszul duality patterns in representation theory + more
  • Hecke algebras and rational Cherednik algebras
Any proposal is welcome!
